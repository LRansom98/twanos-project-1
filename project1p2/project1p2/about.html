About Us

Mission Statement

Our mission is to guarantee tasty, homestyle, prepared meals for
milennials at a moderate cost.



Vision Statement

Our vision is to ensure affordable, quality, home-cooked style meals
that will satisfy the taste buds of college students as well as local residents.
They will be they will be offered dishes with guaranteed flavor that
will make them regret eating in the cafeteria.



Biography

Twano's began just as a small idea of the owner, Stantwan Hunter, who
was a college scholar that was tired of eating the same thing in the school's
cafeteria. With his bright idea, he used the cooking skills that he learned
from his mother to create what is now the greatest company in the food industry!
